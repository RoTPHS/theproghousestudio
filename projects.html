Rodrigo Peralta

Musician/Composer/Music Producer. Guitarist and composer for over 15 years, I have 2 progressive metal projects: APICE and Australs. I started composing for video games in 2022. Since 2020, I have my home studio (The Prog House Studio) where I dedicate myself to producing and composing music in general, whether for video games, musical or audiovisual projects.

Australs

Instrumental Prog Metal from Argentina. Founded in 2020, in the middle of the pandemic, by Rodrigo Peralta (APICE). Until today it has 2 EPs, "To Prevail" and the recent "2nd Universe". Always with the intention of creating music with which the listener can imagine his own story.

APICE

APICE is a Progressive Metal band from Argentina, formed in 2015 by Rodrigo Peralta (guitar). It has a sound that is more melodic than technical, incorporating classical and modern prog elements. Until today released the single "Ruinas" in 2017 and the instrumental demo "The Homemade Demo" in 2018, composed entirely of its guitarists Alejo Delgado and Rodrigo Peralta.
